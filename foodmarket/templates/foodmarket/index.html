WHAT DO WE DO?

Operation Almond is a platform for buying and selling home-made food, from cookies to sandwiches to smoothies. Chefs can list food for sale in their kitchen, while customers can browse foods that have been listed. Payment will be processed with venmo, so please create an account if you don't already have one.
